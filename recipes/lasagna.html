Delicious Homemade Lasagna

Lasagna Recipe

This recipe was passed down from my grandma's grandma. It has been in my family for years and I am so excited to share it with you. I hope you enjoy eating it as much as I have.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Tomato sauce
  • Lasagna
  • Garlic Salt
  • Tomato paste

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef in a pan on medium to high heat.
  2. While cooking the beef add garlic salt.
  3. Once the beef has been fully cooked, mix in a tube of tomato paste.
  4. Pre-heat your oven to 400 degrees.
  5. Place a single layer of the lasagna in a cooking dish.
  6. Add a layer of cheese.
  7. Add an even layer of the cooked beef.
  8. Add an aditional layer of cheese mixed with diced tomatoes.
  9. Place another layer of lasagna and repeate steps 6-8 until satisfied.
  10. Onece the final layer of lasagna has been placed, add tomato sauce and cheese to the top of the lasagna.
  11. Place in the oven and bake for 20 minutes.
