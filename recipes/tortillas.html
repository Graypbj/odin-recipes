Homemade Tortillas

There is nothing better than having some fresh tortillas. This recipe will teach you how to make hot and ready tortillas.

Ingredients

  • 4 Cups of Flour
  • 2 Teaspoons of Baking Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 2 Tablespoons of Lard
  • 1 1/2 Cup of Water

Steps

  1. Begin by mixing the flour, baking powder, and salt together.
  2. Next, using your hands, knead the lard into the mixture.
  3. The mixture will be dry like cornmeal, now we can add water until the dough comes together.
  4. Divide the flour into small balls and roll the out using a rolling pin.
  5. Now place the tortillas on a preheated skillet and cook until golden.
