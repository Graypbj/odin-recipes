Mama's Steak Tacos

Delicious Caribbean Tacos

These delicious steak tacos are straight from the Caribbean. This recipe combines an amazing assortement of spices and seasoning with the irrisistable flavor of lime.

Ingredients

  • Flour (Corn or Wheat)
  • Salt
  • 1 Pound of Steak
  • Ground Pepper
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Lime
  • Paprika
  • Chili Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Cumin
  • Baking Powder
  • Lard
  • Water
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Begin by following this recipe to make your tortillas.
  2. Now we can begin by seasoning our steak meat.
    1. Begin by cutting up the steak meat into half inch slices.
    2. Next, place 2 tablespoons of salt in a small bowl
    3. Add about a tablespoon of Chili powder
    4. A pinch of cumin
    5. A teaspoon of garlic powder
    6. Half a teaspoon of ground pepper
    7. Mix the bowl up.
    8. Add the steak meat to the bowl and mix away, making sure that the steak meat is covered.
  3. Now that the steak meat is seasoned, we can begin cooking it.
  4. Pour a tablespoon of olive oil in a pan and heat on medium to high heat
  5. Add the steak meat once the oil is warm enough.
  6. Once the steak meat is cooked, place the meat in the tortillas.
